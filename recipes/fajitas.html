Fajitas Recipe

This is a simple fajitas recipe using only a few ingredients. This recipe serves 2-3, takes 45 minutes to complete and requires an oven, mixing bowl and baking sheet.

Ingredients

  • 4 bell peppers
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb. chicken tenderloins
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp coriander
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Slice bell peppers, onion and chicken into 1/4 inch-wide strips. Cut chicken against the grain.
  2. Toss peppers & onions in 1.5 Tbsp of olive oil. Bake for 8 minutes on a sheet pan.
  3. Combine chicken, spices, and 1.5 Tbsp olive oil in a mixing bowl. Add to sheet pan and bake for 15-17 minutes.
