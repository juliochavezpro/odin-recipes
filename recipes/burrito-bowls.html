Burrito Bowls Recipe

This is a simple burrito bowl recipe using only a few ingredients. This recipe serves two, takes 30 minutes to complete and requires a stove, pot, and saucepan.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 poblano pepper
  • 1/4 cup mexican blend shredded cheese
  • 2 Tbsp sour cream
  • 1/2 packet of taco seasoning
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Slice your green onion, separating white slices from green slices. Chop your tomato and poblano pepper. Set aside.
  2. Add 2 tbsp of olive oil to your pot and the white slices of your green onion. Bring high heat and cook green onion slices until tender.
  3. Add water and salt to your pot and bring to a boil. Add rice and return to a boil. Once boiling, cover your pot and cook the rice at low heat for 25 minutes, adding butter when finished.
  4. While rice cooks, add your tomato and remaining green onion slices to a small bowl. Add salt, pepper and remaining olive oil and mix. Set aside until ready to serve.
  5. When 10 minutes remain for the rice to cook, heat your saucepan to medium high and add 1 tbsp and your chopped poblano pepper. Cook until pepper is tender.
  6. Drain your black bean can, then add your beans to the saucepan. Add add remaining butter, taco seasoning, and salt and pepper to taste. Cook for 6 minutes or until beans thicken.
  7. To serve, add rice to bowl then top with beans, shredded cheese, tomato/green onion mixture, and sour cream.
